Contribute to P2:15

Currently, our open-source, non-profit Bible Study App is maintained by just two contributors. We're a scrappy team with minimal infrastructure, so getting started might be a bit challenging. However, we believe this project has the potential to significantly help people grow closer to God, and we'd love your help to make that a reality.​

Embracing the Challenge

We understand that contributing to a project in its early stages can be daunting. Our documentation is still a work in progress, and our development environment may require some patience to navigate. Despite these hurdles, we are committed to creating a supportive and collaborative space where contributors can thrive. Your fresh perspectives and skills can help us build a more robust and user-friendly application.

Why Your Contribution Matters

By joining our efforts, you have the opportunity to:

  • Make a Tangible Impact: Play a pivotal role in developing a tool that aids individuals in their spiritual journey.
  • Shape the Project's Direction: Influence the app's features and functionality from the ground up.
  • Grow with the Community: Collaborate with like-minded individuals who share a passion for faith and technology.

Areas Where You Can Help

There are several ways to contribute, depending on your interests and expertise:

  • Development: Assist in coding new features, refining existing ones, or addressing bugs.
  • Documentation: Help us create clear and comprehensive guides to make onboarding smoother for future contributors.
  • Design: Enhance the user interface and overall user experience to make the app more engaging.
  • Community Engagement: Support users by answering questions, gathering feedback, and fostering a welcoming environment.

Getting Started

While we may not have an extensive setup, here are some initial steps to help you begin:

  1. Explore Our Repository: Visit our GitHub to familiarize yourself with the project structure and current issues.
  2. Connect with Us: Reach out via email to introduce yourself and discuss how you’d like to contribute.
  3. Set Up Your Environment: Follow the guidelines provided in our repository to set up the development environment. Feel free to ask questions if you encounter any difficulties.

A Heartfelt Invitation

We recognize that the path may not be perfectly paved, but the destination is worthwhile. Your contributions can help create a meaningful tool that supports individuals in their spiritual growth. Together, we can overcome the initial challenges and build something truly impactful.

If you're ready to embark on this journey with us, please reach out. We look forward to collaborating with you and witnessing the positive change we can achieve together.
